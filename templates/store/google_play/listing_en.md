## Graphic Assets

### App Icon
- Size: 512x512 pixels
- Format: PNG (32-bit with alpha)
- Filename: `icon_512.png`

### Feature Graphic
- Size: 1024x500 pixels
- Format: JPEG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- Filename: `feature_graphic.png`
- Note: Place key content in center 768x500 area

### Screenshots (Phone)
- Size: 1080x1920 (recommended) or 1242x2208
- Format: JPEG or 24-bit PNG
- Count: Min 2, Max 8

#### Recommended Screenshot Composition
1. `screenshot_01_main.png` - Main gameplay
2. `screenshot_02_feature.png` - Key feature/system
3. `screenshot_03_content.png` - Content/characters
4. `screenshot_04_rewards.png` - Rewards/achievements
5. `screenshot_05_social.png` - Social/community features

### Screenshots (7" Tablet)
- Size: 1280x800 or 800x1280
- Count: Min 1

### Screenshots (10" Tablet)
- Size: 1920x1200 or 1200x1920
- Count: Min 1
