## App Store Optimization Tips

### Title Optimization
- Use your main keyword in the title
- Keep it memorable and easy to spell
- Avoid generic names

### Keyword Optimization
- Research competitor keywords
- Use specific, relevant terms
- Avoid spaces after commas
- Test and iterate

### Screenshot Optimization
- First 2 screenshots are most important
- Show actual gameplay
- Add text overlay to highlight features
- Use consistent visual style

### Review Management
- Respond to all reviews professionally
- Address negative feedback constructively
- Thank positive reviewers
- Use SKStoreReviewController wisely
